Cookie Notice

This notice explains what cookies are, how we use them on the Goodfellow Finance website, and how you can manage your preferences. For broader information about how we handle personal data, see our Privacy & Data Protection Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and may remember your preferences.

2. Cookies we use

TypePurposeDuration
Essential / consentRemembers your cookie consent choice (goodfellow_cookie_consent in localStorage)Until cleared
FunctionalSupports basic site navigation and form functionalitySession
Third-party (Google Fonts)Loads web fonts — Google may collect IP address and usage dataPer Google policy
Third-party (Google Maps)Embedded map on our Contact page — Google may set cookies when the map loadsPer Google policy

We do not currently use third-party advertising or analytics cookies on this website.

3. Managing cookies

When you first visit our site, a banner asks you to accept cookies or read this notice. You can also:

  • Clear cookies and site data through your browser settings
  • Block third-party cookies in your browser
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

Blocking essential cookies may affect how some parts of the website work.

4. Updates

We may update this Cookie Notice when we change how our website uses cookies. The effective date at the top will be revised accordingly.
