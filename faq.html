General Questions

What is Goodfellow Finance Limited?

Goodfellow Finance Limited is an indigenous Zambian microfinance institution registered with the Bank of Zambia. Established in 2017, we specialize in providing payday salary advances and short-term loans to employed individuals across all sectors. We operate 28 branches nationwide, serving customers in all 10 provinces of Zambia.

Who can apply for a loan at Goodfellow Finance?

We serve employed individuals from various sectors including government employees, mining sector workers, ZESCO employees, NGO staff, agricultural sector employees, teachers, nurses, private sector employees, and small to medium enterprise (SME) owners. You must have a regular source of income and meet our basic eligibility criteria.

What types of loans do you offer?

We offer two main types of loans:

  • Payday Salary Advances: Quick short-term loans for salaried employees
  • Short-Term Loans: Flexible loans with terms of 1-2 months

All our loans feature competitive interest rates and are designed to meet your immediate financial needs.

Application Process

How do I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan through three convenient methods:

  • Walk-In: Visit any of our 28 branches across Zambia
  • Online: Apply through our website using the online application form
  • Mobile Phone: Contact us via phone for application assistance

Our application process is simple and designed for your convenience.

What documents do I need to apply?

To apply for a loan, you'll typically need:

  • Valid National Registration Card (NRC)
  • Proof of employment (employment letter or payslip)
  • Bank account details
  • Contact information

Additional documents may be required depending on your employment sector and loan type.

How long does it take to get approved?

We pride ourselves on quick approval times! Most loan applications are reviewed and approved on the same day, provided all required documents are submitted and verified. You'll receive notification of your approval status via phone call, SMS, or email.

Disbursement & Repayment

How will I receive my loan?

We offer flexible disbursement options to suit your needs:

  • Instant Cash: Collect cash from any of our branches
  • Bank Transfer: Direct deposit to your bank account
  • Mobile Money: Transfer to your mobile money wallet

You can choose the method that's most convenient for you during the application process.

How do I repay my loan?

We use two secure repayment methods:

  • DDACC (Direct Debit Authority Collection): Automatic deduction from your bank account on the agreed date
  • PMEC (Payroll Management and Employee Contributions): Direct deduction from your salary by your employer

These automated systems ensure you never miss a payment and maintain a good credit record.

What are the loan terms and repayment period?

Our short-term loans typically have repayment periods of 1-2 months. The specific terms, including the repayment schedule and interest rate, will be clearly explained and agreed upon before you sign the loan agreement. We believe in transparency and ensuring you understand all loan terms.

Rates & Fees

What are your interest rates?

We offer competitive interest rates that vary depending on the loan type, amount, and repayment period. Our rates are in full compliance with Bank of Zambia regulations. Contact us or visit any of our branches for detailed rate information tailored to your specific loan requirements.

Are there any hidden fees?

No! We believe in complete transparency. All fees, charges, and interest rates are clearly disclosed in your loan agreement before you sign. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. What you see is what you pay.
