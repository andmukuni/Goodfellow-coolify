Our Financial Solutions

Goodfellow Finance Limited offers a range of financial services tailored to meet the needs of public and private sector employees across Zambia

Payday Salary Advances

Quick and convenient salary advances for employees in both public and private sectors. Get instant access to your salary before payday with competitive rates and flexible 1-2 month repayment terms through DDACC or PMEC recovery.

Short-Term Loans

Affordable short-term loans designed to help you meet immediate financial needs. With competitive rates and quick approval, our loans provide the financial support you need when you need it most.

Public Sector Services

Specialized financial solutions for government employees, including various ministries, Zambia Army, Zambia Air Force, Zambia National Service, and parastatal organizations. Trusted by thousands of public servants nationwide.

Private Sector Services

Tailored financial services for employees in mining companies, manufacturing, ZESCO, NGOs, and higher learning institutions. We understand the unique needs of private sector employees and offer customized solutions.

Agricultural Sector

Financial support for workers in the agricultural sector. We believe in diversification of income generating opportunities for sustainable wealth creation and support agricultural development across Zambia.

SME Support

Financial assistance for Small to Medium Enterprises. We empower entrepreneurs and business owners with the capital they need to grow their businesses and create sustainable wealth for their communities.
