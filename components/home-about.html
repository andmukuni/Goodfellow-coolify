Who We Are

Goodfellow Finance Limited (GFL) is an indigenous Zambian Microfinance institution established and registered with the Bank of Zambia in 2017. We strive to add value to the Zambian economy through provision of financial services to individuals in both the public and private sector.

We entered the market with a proven record of expertise by our Management teams who have worked in the Banking Sector, other Financial Institutions, Civil Service and Private Sector industry for more than 15 to 20 years. Our primary focus lies on client satisfaction and timely approval of all relevant documentation to shorten the bureaucratic process.

Bank of Zambia Registered

Officially registered and licensed microfinance institution since 2017, ensuring your financial security and peace of mind.
