Our Resolution Process

1

Complaint Receipt

Timeline: Immediate

We acknowledge receipt of your complaint immediately and provide you with a reference number for tracking purposes.

2

Investigation

Timeline: 1-3 Business Days

Our dedicated team investigates your complaint thoroughly, gathering all relevant information and documentation.

3

Resolution Proposal

Timeline: Within 5 Business Days

We contact you with our findings and proposed resolution. Most complaints are resolved at this stage.

4

Final Resolution

Timeline: Within 14 Business Days (Maximum)

If you accept our proposal, we implement the resolution. If not satisfied, we provide information about escalation options.

Committed Timeline

As per Bank of Zambia regulations, we aim to resolve all complaints within 14 business days. Complex cases may require additional time, but we will keep you informed throughout the process.

If You're Not Satisfied - Escalation Options

Step 1: Senior Management

If you're not satisfied with the initial resolution, request escalation to our senior management team.

  • Contact: Senior Complaints Officer
  • Email: customercare@goodfellow.co.zm
  • Timeline: Additional 5 business days

Step 2: Bank of Zambia

If still unresolved, you have the right to lodge your complaint with the Bank of Zambia.

What to Include in Your Complaint

Personal Information
  • Your full name
  • Account or loan number
  • Contact information (phone, email)
  • National Registration Card number
Complaint Details
  • Clear description of the issue
  • Date and time of incident
  • Branch or staff involved (if applicable)
  • Supporting documents or evidence
Helpful Tip

The more detailed information you provide, the faster we can investigate and resolve your complaint. Keep copies of all correspondence for your records.

Your Consumer Rights

Protected by Bank of Zambia Regulations

Right to Complain

You have the right to lodge a complaint without fear of retaliation or negative consequences.

Timely Response

You have the right to receive acknowledgment and resolution within specified timelines.

Clear Information

You have the right to clear information about our complaint process and your options.

Fair Treatment

You have the right to impartial investigation and fair resolution of your complaint.

Escalation Options

You have the right to escalate unresolved complaints to Bank of Zambia.

Confidentiality

Your complaint and personal information will be handled with strict confidentiality.

Privacy & data protection complaints

If your complaint relates to how we handle your personal data and you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner under the Data Protection Act, 2021. Visit www.dataprotection.gov.zm or read our Privacy & Data Protection Policy.
